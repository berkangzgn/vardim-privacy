Privacy Policy

Shift Tracking System
👋

Overview

Vardim is a shift tracking application designed to help you manage and monitor your work schedule. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

Vardim does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.
📋

Information We Collect

Vardim does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app stores your shift settings and preferences locally on your device only. This includes:

· Your selected shift group (A, B, C, or D)
· Shift cycle definitions and working hours
· Notification preferences
· App settings and configurations

None of this information is transmitted to us or any third party.

🔐

Permissions Used

🔔
Notifications

Used to send you shift reminders before your shift begins. You can enable or disable this at any time in the app settings or your device settings. No notification data is shared with third parties.

📅
Calendar

Used to display your shift schedule within the app's calendar view. We do not read, modify, or access your personal calendar events. Calendar data remains entirely on your device.

🌐

Third-Party Services

Vardim does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

👶

Children's Privacy

Vardim does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not target or appeal to children.

📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
