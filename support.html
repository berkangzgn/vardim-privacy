Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my shift calendar showing incorrect days?

Make sure the start date in Settings matches the first day of your current shift cycle. An incorrect start date will cause the entire schedule to be offset.

I am not receiving shift notifications.

Go to your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Vardim and make sure notifications are allowed. Also check that "Send Notification" is enabled inside the app's Settings screen.

Can I track multiple shift groups at the same time?

Yes. Vardim supports A, B, C, and D groups simultaneously. You can view all groups on the calendar by enabling "Show Other Groups" in Settings.

How do I change my shift cycle duration?

Open Settings → Shift Cycle Definitions and use the +/- buttons to adjust the number of working days and days off for each shift type.

Is my data backed up to the cloud?

No. All data is stored locally on your device only. This ensures your privacy but also means data will be lost if you delete the app. We recommend noting your settings before reinstalling.

How do I reset all data?

Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete All Data". This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
